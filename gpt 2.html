About VIT Pune

Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT) Pune, established in 1983, is one of Maharashtra's leading private engineering colleges. It is an autonomous institute affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University and accredited with NAAC 'A++' Grade. The institute is also approved by AICTE.

VIT Pune aims to provide high-quality technical education and foster research and innovation. The institute has well-equipped laboratories, a vast library, and modern classrooms. It emphasizes holistic development by encouraging students to participate in extracurricular activities and community service.

VIT Pune has a strong focus on industry collaboration, offering students various opportunities for internships, workshops, and real-world projects. The institute hosts numerous technical and cultural events, providing a vibrant campus life.

Academics

VIT Pune offers a variety of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. Some of the popular courses include B.Tech, M.Tech, and Ph.D. The institute is known for its academic excellence and research opportunities.

The academic programs are designed to equip students with theoretical knowledge and practical skills. The curriculum is regularly updated to meet industry standards and technological advancements.

Faculty members at VIT Pune are highly qualified and experienced, dedicated to guiding students in their academic and professional pursuits. The institute also offers various scholarships and financial aid programs to support deserving students.

Placements

VIT Pune has a strong placement record with top companies like TCS, Accenture, Capgemini, and L&T Infotech visiting the campus. The highest package offered last year was 44 LPA with an average package of 7.5 LPA. Around 85% of students secure placements.

The institute has a dedicated placement cell that provides career guidance, training, and placement assistance to students. Regular workshops, mock interviews, and aptitude tests are conducted to prepare students for the recruitment process.

VIT Pune also has a strong alumni network, which plays a significant role in mentoring current students and providing industry insights. Many alumni hold prominent positions in various organizations and contribute to the institute's growth.

Campus Life

The campus is equipped with modern facilities and offers a vibrant student life with various activities and events. The institute also has an online education platform called VOLP for e-classroom learning.

VIT Pune encourages students to participate in clubs, societies, and sports activities. The campus hosts annual cultural and technical fests, providing students with opportunities to showcase their talents and skills.

The institute has various student support services, including health and wellness programs, counseling services, and accommodation facilities. The campus is eco-friendly and promotes sustainable practices.
