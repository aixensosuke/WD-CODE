Dance Club

The Dance Club is a dynamic group of students passionate about expressing themselves through movement and rhythm. Key features include:

  • Regular workshops on popular dance forms like hip-hop, contemporary, and classical.
  • Performance opportunities at college fests, competitions, and cultural events.
  • Collaborations with professional choreographers for skill enhancement.
  • An inclusive environment for beginners and experienced dancers alike.

Coding Club

The Coding Club is the go-to community for tech enthusiasts aiming to sharpen their programming skills. Highlights include:

  • Weekly coding sessions focusing on algorithms, data structures, and problem-solving.
  • Hackathons and coding competitions to foster innovation and teamwork.
  • Guest lectures by industry experts and alumni from top tech companies.
  • Guidance for internships, technical interviews, and open-source contributions.

Gaming Club

The Gaming Club is the perfect hub for gamers looking to connect, compete, and explore the world of gaming. Activities include:

  • Regular tournaments for popular games like Valorant, CS: GO, FIFA, and more.
  • Gaming workshops for beginners, including tips and tricks for competitive play.
  • Community meetups for casual gaming and discussions on industry trends.
  • A dedicated Discord server for members to connect and organize matches.
