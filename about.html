About VIT Pune



Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT), Pune, is a premier engineering institute located in Pune, Maharashtra, India. Established in 1983, VIT Pune has consistently been at the forefront of imparting quality technical education and fostering innovation. The institute is affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU) and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

VIT Pune offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various engineering and technology disciplines. The institute is dedicated to nurturing talent and promoting research and development. VIT Pune's curriculum is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills required to excel in their respective fields. The faculty comprises experienced and highly qualified professionals who are committed to academic excellence.

The campus is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories, modern classrooms, and extensive library facilities. These resources provide students with the best possible learning environment. The institute also emphasizes extracurricular activities, encouraging students to participate in sports, cultural events, and technical competitions. This holistic approach ensures the overall development of students, making them well-rounded individuals.
