Robotics Club

Robotics

This forum was formed to help the students interested in robotics and to participate in various robotics competitions like ABU RoboCon, e-yantra. They conduct two workshops during each academic year which is only focused only robotics. Students from any branch can join if you only want to learn about robotics (like micro-controller programming kind of stuff etc.)..
